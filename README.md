# Zepto-E-commerce-SQL-Data-Analyst-Project-

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes Zepto product inventory data using PostgreSQL.  
The dataset was collected from Kaggle in CSV format and imported into PostgreSQL for data cleaning, exploration, and business analysis.

---

## 📂 Dataset
Dataset taken from Kaggle in CSV format.

# 🔍 Data Exploration
- Counted total records in the dataset.
- Viewed sample product data.
- Identified null values in important columns.
- Extracted distinct product categories.
- Compared in-stock vs out-of-stock products.
- Detected duplicate product names.

---

# 🧹 Data Cleaning
- Identified products with zero pricing values.
- Removed invalid records from the dataset.
- Converted product prices from paise to rupees.

---

# 📊 Data Analysis Queries

### 🏷️ Q1. Top 10 Products with Highest Discount
Identified products offering the highest discount percentages.

### 📦 Q2. Expensive Products Out of Stock
Found high MRP products currently unavailable in stock.

### 💰 Q3. Estimated Revenue by Category
Calculated estimated revenue generated by each product category.

### 🛍️ Q4. Products with High MRP and Low Discount
Filtered premium products with discounts below 10%.

### 📈 Q5. Top Categories with Highest Average Discount
Analyzed categories providing the best average discounts.

### ⚖️ Q6. Price Per Gram Analysis
Calculated price per gram for products above 100g to identify better value products.

### 🏷️ Q7. Product Weight Classification using CASE
Grouped products into Low, Medium, and Bulk categories based on weight.

### 📦 Q8. Total Inventory Weight Per Category
Calculated total inventory weight available in each category.

### 🔎 Q9. Products with Above Average MRP (Subquery)
Used subquery to identify products priced above average MRP.

### 🏆 Q10. Product Ranking Based on Discount (Window Function)
Applied RANK() window function to rank products according to discount percentage.

### 🔗 Q11. Product and Category Discount Analysis (JOIN)
Used INNER JOIN to compare products with average category discounts.
